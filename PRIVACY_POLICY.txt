Tambola Caller – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

• Tambola Caller runs entirely on your device. Game state (e.g. which numbers were called) and app settings are stored only on your device.
• We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
• Voice features use your device's built-in text-to-speech; no audio is sent to our servers (we have no servers).
• If you contact us (e.g. via the Support URL), we will use your contact details only to respond.

For questions, use the Support URL listed in the app.
